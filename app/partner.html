Membership Applications

At Killing Kittens, we believe that membership should not be based on external factors such as looks, wealth, influence, body shape, age, ability, sexual orientation, or connections. Instead, it's all about your mindset and approach.

We are seeking out individuals who are sex-positive and open to discovering new pleasures. Our members prioritise consent and are willing to explore their fantasies, challenge their limits, and try new things.

Our team carefully reviews each application to ensure that it is genuine, sincere, and aligned with our mission of empowerment and liberation. If anything seems fraudulent, insincere, or not in line with our values, we will not proceed with the application.

We may also ask applicants to provide references from other Killing Kittens members in support of their application.
