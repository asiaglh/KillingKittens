THE RULES

We are determined to give safer spaces to women, transgender and non-binary people to access exploration, to discover pleasure and to find joy in their bodies, identities and sexualities. The following explains how this ethos is put into practise at our parties, as well as outlining some other basic rules.

  1. Only Killing Kittens Members can buy tickets, and have the ability to bring one guest with them.
  2. Yes to consent, no to creeps. Everyone, regardless of gender identity, must practise consent at our parties. You can read the full guidelines here.
  3. Men, you are welcome at our parties, but consider how your presence impacts a space, and be mindful of how you approach and interact with others. Any kind of toxic masculinity will not be tolerated at our events.
  4. Be respectful and considerate. Do not make any assumptions about the gender or physiology of those around you. Feel free to introduce yourself with your pronouns, if you feel comfortable doing so.
  5. No phones or unauthorised photography.
  6. No drugs. Anyone found with or using illegal drugs will be ejected from the party and reported to the police. Our door team will be carrying out random bag checks on arrival.
  7. Men cannot enter the playrooms solo or linger alone. They must wait to be invited.
  8. Killing Kittens Members are responsible for their Guests. Members may not leave their Guests behind at the party.
  9. In iconic KK form, all party-attendees must wear their masks until the play spaces open…
  10. Any problems, questions, or concerns, please speak to a member of staff immediately. Our welcoming hosts are easily distinguished by their red armbands and are here to offer judgement-free help, advice, or to have a chat.
